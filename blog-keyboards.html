Best Keyboards for CPS & Spacebar Games

Published on February 2025 by Hitbar Team

If you’re serious about achieving high CPS (Clicks Per Second) scores, having the right keyboard can make a huge difference. Not all keyboards are built for speed, so let’s explore the best options.

1. Mechanical Keyboards

Mechanical keyboards are the top choice for CPS gamers. Switches like Cherry MX Red, Blue, or Brown provide tactile feedback and fast actuation.

2. Low Profile Keyboards

Low profile switches reduce the travel distance, meaning less effort per keypress. This can help you achieve higher CPS with less strain.

3. Keyboards with Anti-Ghosting

Ghosting occurs when multiple keypresses are not registered. A gaming keyboard with anti-ghosting ensures all your clicks count.

4. Compact Keyboards

60% or TKL (Tenkeyless) keyboards are lightweight and allow more comfortable hand positioning for long CPS sessions.

Conclusion

Choosing the right keyboard depends on your comfort and play style. A mechanical keyboard is generally the best investment for serious CPS players.
