Privacy Policy

At Hitbar, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our website and game.

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect personal data such as email or phone numbers.
  • Your game scores are stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
  • Anonymous data may be collected via Google Analytics to improve our site.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve your gaming experience.
  • Maintain leaderboard functionality in your browser.
  • Serve relevant ads via Google AdSense.

Cookies

We may use cookies and third-party services (like Google AdSense and Analytics) to provide a better experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “last updated” date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact Page.
