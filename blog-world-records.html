World Records in CPS & Spacebar Games

Published on February 2025 by Hitbar Team

CPS (Clicks Per Second) challenges are popular worldwide, and some players have achieved unbelievable records that inspire others to push their limits.

1. Highest CPS Recorded

The highest CPS scores often reach above 20 clicks per second, achieved by professional speed clickers using advanced techniques like butterfly clicking.

2. Longest Spacebar Marathon

Some players have set records by pressing the spacebar thousands of times in a row, proving both endurance and dedication.

3. Competitive Events

Online communities frequently organize CPS tournaments where players compete for the highest score under strict rules.

4. How to Compete

You can challenge yourself on platforms like Hitbar and compare your score with global players. Keep practicing, and you might set the next record!

Conclusion

World records show the potential of human speed and persistence. With enough practice, you could join the leaderboard of top CPS players.
